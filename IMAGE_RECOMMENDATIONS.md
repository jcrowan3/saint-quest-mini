# Image Recommendations for Saint Quest

## Saints Portrait Images

### Where to Find Free, Catholic-Appropriate Images:

1. **Wikimedia Commons** (Public Domain)
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/
   - Search for each saint by name
   - Look for paintings, icons, or photographs

2. **Catholic Stock Photo** (Free tier available)
   - https://catholicstock.com/
   - Professional Catholic imagery

3. **Pixabay/Unsplash** (For symbolic images)
   - Can use for background textures, symbols

### Recommended Images to Download:

#### St. Francis of Assisi
- **Search:** "St Francis of Assisi painting"
- **Suggested:** Classical paintings showing him with animals/birds
- **File name:** `francis.jpg`
- **Place in:** `/public/images/saints/`

#### St. Paul the Apostle
- **Search:** "St Paul apostle icon" or "St Paul Damascus"
- **Suggested:** Byzantine icon style or classical painting
- **File name:** `paul.jpg`
- **Place in:** `/public/images/saints/`

#### St. Thérèse of Lisieux
- **Search:** "St Therese Lisieux photograph" or "Little Flower saint"
- **Suggested:** Actual photograph of her (public domain) or painting with roses
- **File name:** `therese.jpg`
- **Place in:** `/public/images/saints/`

#### Blessed Carlo Acutis
- **Search:** "Carlo Acutis photo"
- **Suggested:** His authentic photograph (widely available, often used by Church)
- **File name:** `carlo.jpg`
- **Place in:** `/public/images/saints/`
- **Note:** Carlo's photos are recent and very relatable for kids!

## Quick Way to Add Images:

### Option 1: Download manually
1. Search Google Images with "public domain" or "creative commons"
2. Download each saint's image
3. Save to `/public/images/saints/` folder
4. Name them: `francis.jpg`, `paul.jpg`, `therese.jpg`, `carlo.jpg`

### Option 2: Use placeholder service (temporary)
- Use a service like https://via.placeholder.com/ for now
- Replace with real images later

### Option 3: AI-Generated (if appropriate)
- Could use AI art generators for stylized, consistent look
- Make sure they're appropriate and recognizable
